Unemployment statistics : age group comparison

AGE 18-40

Unemployment rate for age 18-40 was 5.10% in December of 2023, according to the EUROSTAT. FTE rate remained stable (approx 43%).

AGE 41-65

Unemployment rate for age 41-65 was 5.90% in December of 2023, according to the EUROSTAT. FTE rate increased for older people (from 56 % to 60 %).
